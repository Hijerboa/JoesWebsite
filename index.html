What We Do



Buy


Did you just buy a new computer? Have an old computer lying around you no longer need?
Well you're in luck! We buy used computers and laser printers for a fair and competitive price (we don't buy inkjet printers). Just bring in your computer and one of our technicians will give you a quote for your computer.
We buy computers in good condition which are running Windows 7, 8, or 10. Older computers sometimes may have value for parts.
If you are buying a new computer from us, we also provide additional trade in value!

Sell


Is your current computer just not cutting it anymore? Are you looking for something, better, faster, stronger?
As a Microsoft Certified Refurbisher, we sell a wide range of computers and laser printers to serve everyone's needs, fitting all budgets and all types of users. You can browse our computer selection online anytime, or stop in and one of our technicians will be able to assist you in your purchase and to help you get the best possible bang for your buck.
