About Us


Our Mission: To serve the Greater Cleveland area with the best parts and services while maintaining a friendly and professional attitude.




Joe's Lakewood Computer is an owner operated computer shop serving Lakewood and the surrounding west-side suburbs.

We specialize in PC, Laptop, & HP LaserJet Printer repair (we do not service Ink Jet printers), as well as technical support for companies and individuals.



hi

We buy both used computers and Laser printers, as well as sell refurbished computers and printers at a fair price.

We also have an expansive inventory of aftermarket toner cartridges, which we deliver to local businesses for free, as well as a small selection of ink cartridges.



In addition to computer repair, computer parts, and selling computers, we specialize in HP Laser printers. If your laser printer is having problems, and you need a repair, we are confident that we will repair it in a quick and timely manner.

We have daily in store deals, and competitive prices. If you have any computer related questions, feel free to stop in anytime and we will surely be able to help solve any of your computer woes.
